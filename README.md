📈 Stock Market Live Analysis Dashboard

📝 Purpose

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze live stock market data. 
The dashboard provides insights into market performance, price trends, trading volume,
volatility, sector-wise performance, and investor sentiment, helping users make data-driven investment decisions.

* 🛠️ Tech Stack
* ⚡ Power BI
* 🐍 Python
* 🐼 Pandas
* 🔢 NumPy
* 📊 DAX
* 🔄 Power Query
* 📈 Yahoo Finance API (yfinance)

📂 Data Source

Live stock market data collected using the Yahoo Finance API (yfinance) and processed using Python before visualization in Power BI.

✨ Features / Highlights
Real-time stock market monitoring
• Market overview with KPIs (Average Price, Maximum Price, Minimum Price, Trading Volume)
• Daily and monthly stock price trend analysis
• Trading volume analysis across months
• Price volatility analysis
• Company-wise stock performance comparison
• Bullish vs Bearish market sentiment analysis
• Sector-wise performance and volatility analysis
• Interactive filters and slicers for dynamic reporting

🎯 Business Problem
Investors and analysts often struggle to monitor multiple stocks, identify market trends, and compare sector performance in real time.
Manual analysis is time-consuming and makes it difficult to identify investment opportunities quickly.

💡Project Key Insights
📊 Market Overview

The dashboard provides an overview of market performance with a 74B total trading volume, reflecting strong market activity.
The average closing price was 2.22K, with prices ranging from 47.80 to 11.58K. Monthly trends show a gradual decline in average closing prices,
while trading volume peaked in January and July, indicating active investor participation.

📈 Price & Volatility Analysis

This dashboard analyzes stock performance and market volatility across companies.
TATAELXSI recorded the highest average closing price, while RVNL showed the highest price growth. 
Volatility was highest in June and July, and the analysis highlights the relationship between trading volume, stock prices, and market fluctuations.

📉 Trend & Sector Analysis

The market showed a slightly bullish sentiment (55.93%) compared to bearish (44.07%). 
The IT sector recorded the highest volatility and stock contribution, followed by the Railways sector.
Most stocks were categorized as high trading volume with low overall volatility, indicating stable market participation.
